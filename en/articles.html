SSL Certificate: Why Your Website Needs It

An SSL certificate encrypts data between the server and the visitor. Without it, the browser marks the site as 'Not secure' and Google lowers it in rankings. Modern hosting providers offer SSL for free through Let's Encrypt. On GitHub Pages, SSL is enabled automatically when you connect a custom domain. Make sure your site is served over HTTPS — it's a mandatory minimum.
